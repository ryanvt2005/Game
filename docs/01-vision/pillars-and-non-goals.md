# Pillars and Non-Goals

## Pillars
1. **Feel first:** camera + movement + impact must be excellent.
2. **Cinematic clarity:** readable VFX, strong silhouettes, distinct abilities.
3. **Systemic story:** heroes fight heroes for coherent reasons.
4. **Small zones, big moments:** reuse environments with changing conditions.
5. **Web performance discipline:** stylized realism, controlled budgets.

## Non-Goals (MVP)
- Open world
- PvP multiplayer
- Photoreal graphics
- Deep crafting/economy
- Massive hero roster
