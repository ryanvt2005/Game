# Factions

## The Vanguard (Order)
Belief: power must be controlled to protect society.
Tactics: containment zones, enforcers, suppression teams.

## The Unbound (Freedom)
Belief: power is evolution; control is oppression.
Tactics: hit-and-run, volatile abilities, rogue cells.

## The Dominion (Villains)
Belief: power is leverage.
Tactics: resource capture, corruption tech, lieutenants and bosses.
