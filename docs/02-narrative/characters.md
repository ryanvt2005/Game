# Characters (MVP Roster)

## Design Safety Checklist (must pass)
- Unique silhouette
- Unique palette (avoid iconic combos)
- Distinct power source/explanation
- At least one unique mechanical twist
- Original name + symbol language

## Playable Heroes (Act I)
### Aegis Vector (Tech Defender)
Role: Balanced/Control
Signature: directional energy planes, tactical defense

### Riftcaller (Energy Manipulator)
Role: DPS/Control
Signature: fracture bolts + spatial distortion

### Kinetic Saint (Momentum Brawler)
Role: Melee/Mobility
Signature: momentum scaling, dash chains, impact shocks
